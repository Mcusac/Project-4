The Car Price Calculator is designed to predict the approximate auction price of a selected Make, Year, Engine (CC), Mileage, and CarFax History. The Car Price Calculator makes use of prior BringATrailer.com auctions to generate a price prediction.

Our model is based on two different supervised machine learning models. Extra Trees Regressor and Random Forest models are ran individually, and the predictions are averaged to generate a price prediction.
